About Me

Brady Kuhn

I am a senior Electrical Engineering student at Iowa State University with an academic focus on circuit and PCB design, electromagnetics, and nondestructive evaluation. From my time working at the Iowa State Electronics and Technology Group, I've developed a strong foundation in PCB design, assembly, and troubleshooting. I am excited to continue my journey and begin my career when I graduate in May 2026.
